Who is WEST-STRND ECO

A youth based waste management and recycling SMME, our commitment to green sustainability drives us to provide tailor-made waste management solutions that cater to the unique needs of our clients. This while creating job opportunities primarily for youth and local recyclers. We service commercial, corporate and industrial establishments, events, hotels, restaurants, shopping centers and educational institutions.

Our Core business

West-STRND ECO is dedicated to transforming waste management while creating social impact. Our business focuses on job creation (32.4%) for youth and older citizens, offering employment opportunities in areas affected by high unemployment.

Community development makes up 24.3% of our efforts, engaging residents in recycling education and local initiatives.

We dedicate 16.2% to recycling services, offering efficient waste management solutions. Buy-back and separation bays make up 13.5%, incentivizing waste sorting.

Lastly, 13.5% focuses on industrial and e-waste recycling, promoting eco-friendly disposal. Together, these pillars build a circular economy that benefits both people and the environment.

  • 32.4% - Job Creation
  • 24.3% - Community Development
  • 16.2% - Recycling Services
  • 13.5% - Buy-Back Hubs
  • 13.5% - industrial & E-Waste Recycling
