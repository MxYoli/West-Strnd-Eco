Welcome To WEST-STRND ECO

Collaborative Social Impact

Partnering with businesses for shared growth. Through collaborations, we expand our reach, engaging more households in responsible recycling practices. Together, we build greener communities, one recyclable item at a time.

Community-Centered Recycling

Revitalizing neighborhoods through localized waste solutions. Our Sorting and Separation Hubs transform illegal dump sites into organized centers, reducing pollution and providing accessible recycling options. Together, we can create cleaner, more sustainable communities.

Inclusive Job Opportunities

Empowering individuals through our Recycling Buy Back Centers. By converting waste into valuable resources, not only reducing pollution but also generating employment in local townships. Every recycled item contributes to economic growth and community development.

Tech-Powered Sustainability

Our STRND'$ app revolutionizes recycling, enabling waste and e-waste pickups, rewarding users, and cultivating environmental stewardship.

    Job & Skills Creation

    Buy Back and Sorting Hub

    The Buy Back and Sorting Hub revolutionizes waste management by creating essential green jobs. It employs local community members to efficiently sort and process recyclables, transforming waste into valuable resources. This initiative not only combats unemployment but also nurtures a sustainable economy, fostering a greener and more prosperous future.

    Tech Development Hub

    The Tech Development Hub is the nucleus of innovation, where the groundbreaking WEST-STRND STRND$ App will take shape in collaboration with esteemed partners: Tsatsi Power, Last Minute Digital, Auko Design, Core, and BizLink Africa. Moreover, it will serve as an inclusive learning space, offering hands-on training in crucial TECH and Engineering skills. From STEAM in Robotics to coding proficiency at any age, and even comprehensive Business Development training, we're committed to nurturing a skilled and empowered workforce for a sustainable future

    Afro Planeteers Youth Hub

    Afro Planeteers are the heart of why we do what we do, they are amazing creatives and innovators that will seed the teachings as we grow and help develop Youth, young and old to learn and also drive our recycling and community street clean drives all while learning and getting mentorship in skills development and Green Jobs, Careers.
